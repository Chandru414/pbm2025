Venue

Ritz-Carlton Dubai International FInancial Centre

The Ritz-Carlton DIFC in the heart of Dubai’s International Financial Centre, surrounded by top restaurants, nightlife, and major business hubs. The hotel offers a direct walkway to The Gate, the vibrant center of DIFC, and is just minutes from landmarks like Dubai Mall and Burj Khalifa. Metro access is excellent, with both Emirates Towers and Burj Khalifa metro stations within a 10-minute walk, and multiple bus lines stopping nearby for easy citywide connectivity. This prime location makes it ideal for both business and leisure travelers seeking convenience and access to Dubai’s key attractions. Sweeping views of the skyline, elegant living spaces, and a magnificent ballroom set in the heart of Dubai’s influential square mile.
